“One District-One Product”, jammu and kashmir

The Jammu and Kashmir Government's One District-One Product Programme promotes unique regional treasures, including prized saffron, Pashmina shawls, walnut woodcraft, papier-mâché art, saffron honey, Kashmiri embroidery, and wickerwork. This initiative celebrates indigenous crafts, fosters economic growth, and preserves cultural heritage.
